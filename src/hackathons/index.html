Why Hackathons?

Learning Happens By Building.

KatCode Hackathons are designed to bridge the gap between theory and practice. Instead of simply learning programming concepts, participants work together to design, build, and present working software within a limited time.

Whether you're writing your very first Java program or preparing for larger software projects, every hackathon is an opportunity to improve your technical skills, collaborate with other developers, and gain practical experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Questions?

Who can participate?

Most KatCode Hackathons are beginner-friendly and designed for students who want to improve their programming skills. Specific eligibility requirements are announced with each event.

Do I need a team?

Some hackathons allow individual registrations while others encourage teamwork. Event details will explain the format.

How do I register?

Registration, team management, submissions, and event updates are handled through the dedicated KatCode Hackathons platform.
